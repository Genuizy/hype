🍄 Truffle Mushroom Risotto

Open Recipe
Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups Arborio Rice
  • 4 cups Warm Vegetable Broth
  • 200g Mixed Wild Mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp Truffle Oil
Preparation Steps
  1. Sauté onions and sliced mushrooms in olive oil.
  2. Add Arborio rice, stirring quickly for 2 minutes.
  3. Slowly add broth until completely absorbed. Fold in truffle oil.
